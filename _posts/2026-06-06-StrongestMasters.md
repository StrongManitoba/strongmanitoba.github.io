# Preamble  
Stratton Agri, Rumble in the Valley & StrongManitoba present: WESTERN CANADA’S STRONGEST MASTER 2026!
Held at the Morris Stampede Grounds in Morris, MB (40 mins south of Winnipeg), join us to find out who’ll take the crown this year! Registration is open and spots are limited!

# Location  
Morris Stampede Grounds, Morris

# Events
- Truck Pull (semi truck for women; truck and trailer for men)  
- Press Medley (barbell, circus dumbbell, axle, log)  
  Women: 135, 70, 155, 165  
  Men: 225, 130, 265, 275  
- Hercules Hold - heavy  
- Deadlift Off (think "Stone off" from WSM)  
  Women: 335  
  Men: 555  
- 5-Stone Run  
  Run finishes with a 265 stone for women; a 365 stone for men
