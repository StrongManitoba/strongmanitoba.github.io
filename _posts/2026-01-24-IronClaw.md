# Preamble  
We are very excited to announce the first competition in the 2026 season: The return of Manitoba’s only grip sport competition, IRON CLAW!

This year StrongManitoba & Manitoba Grip Sport will welcome a very special guest referee, none other than Canada’s Strongest Man, “Sweet T” Tristain Hoath!

Tristain is one of the best strongman competitors to ever come out of Canada, taking 5th at World’s Strongest Man, as well as competing at The Strongest Man on Earth, The Rogue Invitational, and Giants Live!

This will be a 4 lift competition (events TBA), open to anyone and everyone looking to test their grip and find out the best! Iron Claw will also be sanctioned by Grip Sport International, so any Canadian or even world records will be official!

# Location  
Iron Age Strength, Winnipeg

# Events  
- Double-Overhand Axle Deadlift
- Little Big Horn
- Two-Handed Flask
- Finnish Ball
