# Preamble  
Strongman returns to Vita, with PRAIRIES STRONGEST (an SCC sanctioned event)! 

This competition will be refereed by none other than the 3x World Champion, “The Dominator” Inez Carrasquillo!

We’re going old school with this event, over/under 105 kg for the men, and over/under 73 kg for the women (points will be separated afterwards for SCC qualifications)! Masters competitors will be lifting the lightweight weights.

This event is open to Manitobans and non-Manitobans alike, so get registered to compete and meet The Dominator!

# Location  
Shevchenko School, Vita

# Events
- Max Zercher Squat - from the ground  
- Yoke 50' / Log for reps medley  
  Women u73kg: 405 yoke / 115 log  
  Women 73kg+: 455 yoke / 130 log  
  Men u105kg: 600 yoke / 215 log  
  Men 105kg+: 700 yoke / 255 log  
- Throwing Sandbag Run (5 bags)  
  Women u73kg: 15–25  
  Women 73kg+: 15–30  
  Men u105kg: 25–45  
  Men 105kg+: 30–50  
- Death Medley — carry, carry, drag  
- Ascending-weight Farmers Carry (30')  
  Women u73kg: start @ 120, 20 lb jumps  
  Women 73kg+: start @ 170, 20 lb jumps  
  Men u105kg: start @ 210, 50 lb jumps  
  Men 105kg+: start @ 250, 50 lb jumps
