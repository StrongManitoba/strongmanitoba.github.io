# Preamble  
It’s time again to bring out the new faces to Manitoba strongman!

This year the Novice Showdown will be hosted in Winkler by our friends at @the.forgegym, and will feature one of the coolest events in all of strongman, the TRUCK PULL!

This competition is open to anyone with very little to no experience in strongman (if you’re unsure if this is you, contact us), and is additionally open to our regular 50+ competitors!

Registration will be open by next week, so encourage anyone who may be interested to get signed up!

# Location  
The Forge Gym, Winkler

# Events  

- Log Clean and Press for Reps  
  Men: 160 lbs (12" log)  
  Women: 85 lbs (10" log)  
  Teen Boys: 85 lbs (10" log)  
  Teen Girls: 60 lbs (10" log)

- Wagon Wheel (14") Axle Deadlift for Reps  
  Men: 435 lbs  
  Women: 240 lbs  
  Teen Boys: 250 lbs  
  Teen Girls: 175 lbs

- Farmers Carry (30' + 30')  
  Men: 210 lbs/hand  
  Women: 125 lbs/hand  
  Teen Boys: 145 lbs/hand  
  Teen Girls: 85 lbs/hand

- Carry and Load Medley (Sandbags and Kegs)  
  Men: 175 lb SB, 200 lb SB, ~200–225 lb keg  
  Women: 100 lb SB, 125 lb SB, 150 lb keg  
  Teen Boys: 125 lb SB, 150 lb keg, 150 lb SB  
  Teen Girls: 50 lb SB, 100 lb keg, 100 lb SB

- Truck Pull - Heavy
